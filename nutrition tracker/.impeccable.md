## Design Context

### Users
Small group — the developer and friends/family. Health-conscious people who want to track daily nutrition (calories + macros) with minimal friction. They open the app multiple times a day to log meals quickly, glance at progress, and move on. Not power users — the app should require zero learning curve.

### Brand Personality
**Calm, minimal, supportive.** The app stays out of the way. It doesn't lecture, gamify excessively, or demand attention. It's a quiet tool that feels good to use — like Apple Health's composure with occasional warm moments (a gentle celebration when you hit a goal, not a confetti cannon).

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone:** Clean, restrained, premium. White space is a feature, not wasted space.
- **References:** Apple Health/Fitness (rings, minimal chrome, system-native feel) blended with Lose It/Noom's warmth (color, friendly empty states, gentle encouragement).
- **Anti-references:** MyFitnessPal's density and ad clutter. Overly clinical dashboards. Aggressive fitness branding with bold type and neon colors. Generic "AI slop" gradients.
- **Theme:** Light and dark mode, system-preference default. The dark mode should feel as considered as the light mode, not just an inversion.

### Design Principles
1. **Friction-free first** — Every interaction should feel instant. If it takes more than 2 taps to log a food, something is wrong.
2. **Data at a glance** — The dashboard tells the full story without scrolling. Rings, numbers, and color do the work — not labels and explanations.
3. **Quiet confidence** — No exclamation marks in the UI. Animations are subtle and purposeful. The design earns trust through consistency, not flash.
4. **Warm when it matters** — Empty states are encouraging, not blank. Goal completion gets a moment of acknowledgment. Errors are helpful, not scary.
5. **Mobile-native feel** — This is a phone app that happens to run in a browser. Bottom sheets, safe areas, touch targets, and gesture-friendly interactions.

### Established Design Tokens (do not change without reason)
- **Font:** Plus Jakarta Sans (400/500/600/700/800)
- **Macro colors:** Protein #3b82f6 (blue), Carbs #f59e0b (amber), Fat #f43f5e (rose), Calories #10b981 (emerald)
- **Primary accent:** Emerald (buttons, active states, success)
- **Surfaces:** White / #141416 dark, with zinc neutral palette
- **Border radius:** rounded-2xl for cards, rounded-lg for buttons, rounded-full for rings
- **Animation ease:** [0.22, 1, 0.36, 1] custom cubic-bezier, springs at stiffness 400 / damping 25-30
- **Glass effect:** 88% opacity + 20px blur on fixed nav bar
- **Accessibility:** Standard best practices — good contrast, keyboard nav, screen reader support. No specific color blindness or reduced motion requirements beyond defaults.
